Where Color Becomes Feeling

Yehudis paints what cannot be spoken — the luminous interior of sacred moments, the weight of ancestral memory, the quiet intelligence of color and form.

With training in Art Therapy, she sees art as a healing intelligence of its own. Every stroke, every choice of pigment, every composition is an act of witnessing.

About the Artist

Painting as a Path to Wholeness

Color, for Yehudis, is not decoration — it is a medium of the soul. Her training in Art Therapy has given her the understanding that art carries a healing intelligence of its own. Every stroke, every choice of pigment, every composition.
